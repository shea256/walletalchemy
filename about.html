About Us


Our Beginnings

WalletAlchemy was founded by two Princeton students who found it odd that people strapped for cash often have opportunities to reliably buy laptops or armchairs with store credit but may find it quite difficult to get cash at a reasonable interest rate. Sean and Ryan set out to reconcile this apparent gap in the market and bring access to cash at low interest rates to Americans in debt.


What We Do

WalletAlchemy provides short-term financing to debtors in need of a loan at a rate that is significantly more manageable than available in the market today.

Our Mission

To help debtors escape from the debt spiral by providing a low interest rate alternative to payday loans.



Our Team

Sean Pi

Sean started off his career in finance at Guggenheim Partners, where he worked as an analyst for the structured products desk, working closely with the team’s senior CMBS and RMBS traders. Following that, he worked on the restructuring and leveraged finance desks at Jefferies and Company. Prior to joining the team, Sean was an analyst at Citadel Investment Group on their Fundamental Credit desk, working closely with traders on investing in distressed energy and financial credits.

In addition to his corporate experience, Sean has also acted as a freelance consultant for Chinese technology manufacturers (privately owned and state owned) that have been looking to increase their penetration into distribution channels within the United States and South America.

Sean has worked on several solutions to bring financing to debtors in need and is excited to transform an industry that is just waiting to be disrupted.
